History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for developers passionate about open-source technologies. Over the years, it has grown into an international conference that attracts thousands of participants, fostering innovation and collaboration across the tech industry.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to democratize technology education, inspire innovation, and build an inclusive tech community. We strive to provide a platform where developers, entrepreneurs, and tech enthusiasts can learn, share, and grow together.
